Accessibility

Our commitment

We aim to make our website and services accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML and proper labeling for navigation and controls.
  • Color contrast designed for dark themes.
  • Keyboard navigability and focus styles.
  • Alt text for non-decorative images.
  • Continuous improvements based on user feedback.
